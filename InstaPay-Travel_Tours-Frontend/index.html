Mirissa
Mirissa is a beautiful coastal town in Sri Lanka, known for its pristine beaches, whale watching, and vibrant nightlife. A perfect destination for relaxation and adventure.
Adam's Peak
Adam's Peak, also known as Sri Pada, is a sacred mountain in Sri Lanka, famous for its stunning sunrise views and the sacred footprint believed to belong to Buddha, Shiva, or Adam.
Galle Fort
Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a historic fortress in southern Sri Lanka. It is renowned for its well-preserved colonial architecture, cobblestone streets, and scenic ocean views.
